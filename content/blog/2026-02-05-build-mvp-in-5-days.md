Yes — and we do it regularly.

Five years ago it sounded impossible. Today, with the right tools and experience, building a solid MVP in five business days is very realistic. At Pixels and Logic, it's our flagship offer.

We deliver a production-ready MVP including responsive landing pages, contact forms, full authentication (login/register), dev-to-production setup, and complete source code handed over to your GitHub — all in just five business days.

### How It's Possible Today

The secret is a powerful combination of modern frameworks and AI:

- **React** for fast, interactive user interfaces
- **Tailwind CSS** for beautiful, responsive designs in record time
- **Django** for a secure, scalable backend with built-in authentication and admin tools

On top of that, we use the latest AI coding tools to generate components, speed up boilerplate, catch bugs early, and dramatically accelerate development.

What used to take weeks of manual work now gets done in days.

### Our 5-Day MVP Process

Our small, agile team (me with 15 years of experience, developers Ranjit and Kuba, and project manager Ola) follows a tight process:

- **Days 1–2:** Project setup, authentication, and core pages
- **Days 3–4:** Build your unique features with daily progress updates
- **Day 5:** Final testing, deployment, and full code handover

You stay in the loop the entire time and end up with a real, usable product — perfect for validating your idea, showing investors, or getting early customer feedback.
